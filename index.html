Hello and welcome to my Recipe Realm! My name is Maddy and I am endlessly fascinated by the culinary world. Up until about two years ago, I loved to eat but hated to cook, but was always into cooking competition shows and food media in general. I got into cooking out of necessity (eating out is expensive!), and through my exploration of the wealth of cooking content online became obsessed. Just trying is the first step - you are likely abler in the kitchen than you think you are! I aspire to one day find my place in the world of food media, but for now delight in my place in the audience. I love to share the fun recipes I learn with my loved ones, and hope you find some joy and inspiration in what I share, too.

Molly Baz's Miso Braised Chicken and Leeks

If you don't keep miso on stock, you should - far from unapproachable, this paste serves as an instant umami-boost to any recipe it joins. I had never really braised anything before making this, and if you haven't either, you won't regret trying it!

Alison Roman's Skillet Chicken With White Beans and Caramelized Lemon

This recipe opened my eyes to the wonder of bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs - the fat renders from the skin to marry with the lemons and shallots, begetting a sumptuous sauce to coat the beans/kale/chicken. All while the skin has the crispness of dreams!
